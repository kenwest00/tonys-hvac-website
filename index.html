Common Questions

Straight Answers
From the Technician.

How much does AC replacement really cost in Atlanta?
▾
A legitimate full AC system replacement in Atlanta should run $6,000–$14,000 depending on system size, efficiency rating, and installation complexity. If a company is quoting you $20,000–$35,000, you're either looking at unnecessary upgrades being bundled in, or significant overhead being passed to you. Tony's free assessment will tell you exactly what your home needs and what it should cost.
How do I know if I need to replace or just repair my AC?
▾
The general rule: if your system is under 10 years old and the repair is less than 50% of replacement cost, repair it. If it's over 15 years old, has multiple failing components, or uses R-22 refrigerant (phased out), replacement is usually more economical. But every situation is different — that's exactly what Tony's free replacement assessment is for. He'll give you an honest answer, not a sales pitch.
Why are Tony's prices so much lower than competitors?
▾
Large HVAC companies carry overhead that ends up in your quote: salespeople on commission, marketing budgets, management layers, branded trucks, and large physical offices. Tony operates lean and direct. He's not paying a sales team. He's not funding a TV advertising campaign. He does the work himself, and you pay for the work — not the overhead.
What does "free replacement assessment" actually include?
▾
Tony comes to your home, evaluates your current HVAC system's condition, age, efficiency, and remaining lifespan, checks for issues that can be repaired vs. signs of systemic failure, and gives you a clear recommendation: repair, maintain, or replace. If replacement makes sense, he'll quote the job on the spot. The assessment itself is always free, with no obligation to use Tony for the work.
Does Tony do commercial HVAC work or just residential?
▾
Both. Tony serves residential homeowners and commercial clients throughout the Atlanta metro area. If you have a commercial space with HVAC needs, call 770-292-8989 and describe the job — Tony will let you know if it's in his scope and get you a quote.
What is the labor warranty on Tony's work?
▾
Tony backs all of his installations and repairs with a labor warranty. If something he installed or repaired fails due to his workmanship — not a manufacturer defect or unrelated issue — he comes back and makes it right. Ask Tony for the specific warranty terms on your job when you book.
